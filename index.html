About Me

Hello! I'm Taylor MacAnn, an instructional designer passionate about creating impactful and engaging learning solutions. With a strong background in educational technology and a keen eye for user experience, I specialize in transforming complex information into accessible and enjoyable learning journeys. My expertise includes curriculum development, e-learning module creation, multimedia integration, and performance improvement. I believe in a learner-centered approach, ensuring that every design decision contributes to effective knowledge transfer and skill development.
